# Style Pack — saudi-premium

Use for premium regulated-market styling.

Core feel:
- mature
- clean
- dense
- business-oriented
- premium green/gold authority

Recommended:
- base background: dark green-black
- primary accent: amber or gold
- secondary accent: deep green
- cleaner, denser layouts
- best hero variants: `hero-typographic`, `models-tabs`, `tools-carousel`
- good final CTA: `final-cta-dual-card`

Use for:
- mature markets
- business-heavy partnership pages
- pages needing operational trust and polish

Avoid:
- playful casino visuals
- messy gradients
- too much glass
- noisy backgrounds
