# Style Pack — gold-classic

Use when the market needs the canonical gold-on-dark partnership look.

Core feel:
- classic conversion
- dark premium base
- strong gold accent
- high contrast
- direct CTA energy

Recommended:
- base background: near-black
- primary accent: gold
- surfaces: dark frosted or dark accented surfaces
- best hero variants: `hero-split`, `hero-centered`
- good final CTA: `final-cta-panel`, `final-cta-expand`

Use for:
- canonical partner pages
- simple premium betting/casino identity
- markets where gold-on-dark should remain the main brand signal

Avoid:
- making every market look identical
- relying on gold alone for identity
- excessive glass effects
- oversized gaps
